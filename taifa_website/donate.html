Corporate Donor Partners (CDP)

TAIFA LA FURSA currently accepts corporate donations only at the moment. Our Corporate Donor Partners come from organizations and industries of all types and sizes. All of our Corporate Donor Partners are vital to building our programs for Kenyan communities' development and self-sustainability, and we are grateful to each one for their unique contributions.

Strategic Partnerships

TAIFA LA FURSA cultivates innovative collaborations and strategic partnerships with organisations and corporations that share our vision of creating all-inclusive Kenyan communities where people are healthy, educated, productive, creative, innovative, self-reliant, environmentally friendly and socio-economically progressive.

Ambitious Collaborations

Our Corporate Donor Partners represent the most ambitious collaborations that seek to address challenges faced by poor Kenyan communities. We invite you to join us in our quest to promote entrepreneurial and innovative leadership approach to philanthropic social investments as the key to Kenya's restoration and sustainable economic and social transformation.
