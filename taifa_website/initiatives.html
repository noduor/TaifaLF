Our Initiatives

Driving impact through targeted programs and collaborative solutions

Business Networking & Opportunity Matching

TAIFA LA FURSA facilitates structured business networking, investment forums, and opportunity-matching platforms that connect entrepreneurs, SMEs, corporates, financiers, investors, and development institutions.

Through local and international business delegations, trade missions, roundtables, and sector-focused forums, we promote partnerships, joint ventures, market access, and cross-border trade.

Business Investment Networking

Youth Empowerment Initiatives

Our youth programs are designed to equip young people with skills, confidence, and opportunities to participate productively in the economy.

We deliver skills training, mentorship, entrepreneurship support, leadership development, and career pathways, while facilitating access to employment, internships, and enterprise financing.

Youth Training Employment

Women Empowerment Initiatives

TAIFA LA FURSA advances women's economic empowerment and leadership inclusion by supporting women-led enterprises, enhancing access to finance and markets, and strengthening managerial and leadership capabilities.

Our initiatives address structural barriers through targeted training, mentorship, policy engagement, and networking platforms.

Women Empowerment Leadership

Digital Opportunities 24/7 (DO247) Centre

The Digital Opportunities 24/7 (DO247) Centre is a flagship initiative providing continuous access to digital skills development, remote work opportunities, digital entrepreneurship support, online education, and innovation services.

The Centre connects individuals and businesses to opportunities in software development, digital marketing, content creation, data services, AI, e-commerce, consulting, and global digital markets.

Digital Technology Innovation

Sustainable Energy, Agriculture & Environmental Practices

We promote environmentally sustainable and economically viable practices through clean energy adoption, climate-smart agriculture, and environmental conservation initiatives.

Working with schools, training institutions, and community groups, we support tree planting, renewable energy projects, green enterprises, and environmentally responsible income-generating activities.

Environment Sustainability Energy

Food Security through Consolidated Land Practices

TAIFA LA FURSA supports food security and agribusiness growth through land consolidation, cooperative farming models, mechanization, irrigation, value addition, and structured market access.

By enabling farmers to pool land and resources, access finance, and participate in full value chains, we increase productivity, improve incomes, create rural jobs, and contribute to national food security.

Agriculture Food Security Land

Renewed Harmony Mission

The Renewed Harmony Mission is a peace-building and social cohesion initiative that promotes dialogue, reconciliation, ethical leadership, and community restoration.

Through mediation forums, civic education, awareness campaigns, and community-driven restoration projects, the Mission addresses conflict, heals divisions, and strengthens unity and shared national purpose.

Peace Harmony Community

Leadership & Career Empowerment

Our leadership and career initiatives focus on developing competent, ethical, and future-ready leaders across sectors.

We deliver leadership training, career guidance, mentorship, executive development, and international exposure programs, with targeted pathways for youth, women, and professionals to advance into leadership roles.

Leadership Career Development

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Support

TAIFA LA FURSA nurtures innovation and enterprise through incubation and acceleration programs, seed funding, concessional financing, mentorship, research support, exhibitions, and market linkages.

We facilitate support for startups, social enterprises, informal sector innovators, and STEM-based solutions that drive industrial growth, job creation, and inclusive economic development.

Innovation Entrepreneurship Startups
